    Checklist iOS SDK

    Before submitting to Apple, you should make sure that you did everything right and didn’t forget anything.

    Here is our checklist of little things and details you might have forgotten or not tested :

    1. Test with [SmartAdServerView enableTestMode] that all your ads are working fine.

    2. Test with [SmartAdServerView enableLogging] that all your ads are working fine.

    3. Don’t forget to remove [SmartAdServerView enableTestMode] before submitting your app.

    4. Make sure to set all your delegates to nil when your SmartAdServerView objects aren’t used anymore.

    5. Make sure the orientation change is well-handled and that the ad adapts to the screen.

    6. You should test all your interstitial formats with a visible status bar (interstitial, video layer, traveling and bouncing image/video) to check that you put the right frame for the ad. Depending on your application’s architecture, you might have to put 0 or 20 for the y origin as the status bar is sometimes not taken into account in the view frame (for example for a UITabBarController’s view frame).

      We strongly recommend testing with a close button added to the top of the interstitial as it is more obvious when the frame is wrong as the button will be cut and hardly clickable.

    7. If you’re using GPS coordinates, you can check that the right values are passed by checking the body of the request using Fiddler (Windows) or Charles (Mac OS X). Here is how you can use Charles to inspect iPhone web requests on a Mac.

    8. If you want to use video formats, and if you use MPMoviePlayerController objects in your application, make sure that you’ve checked the Video Sample project, especially those two lines of code:

      _theMovie.useApplicationAudioSession = NO;

      [NSTimer scheduledTimerWithTimeInterval:0.1 target:self selector:@selector(addPreRoll) userInfo:nil repeats:NO];
